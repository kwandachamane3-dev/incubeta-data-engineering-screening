In a production setup, I would orchestrate the pipeline as a simple Bronze → Silver → Gold workflow using Dataform or Cloud Composer, with each step dependent on the successful completion of the previous one. The Bronze load would ingest the raw CSV into retail_bronze.raw_transactions, the Silver job would clean and standardize the data into retail_silver.cleaned_transactions, and the Gold job would train the BQML k-means model and write predictions into retail_gold.analytics_customer_segments. For a lighter-weight implementation, these steps could also be run as scheduled queries in BigQuery, with logging and alerting added so failures are visible quickly.
